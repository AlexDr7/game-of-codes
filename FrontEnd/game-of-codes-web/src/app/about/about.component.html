Meet the AI Agents

Vasikia

Vasikia is the first AI agent I implemented. In order to associate words in real time, she looks into wikipedia articles. She finds clues by comparing the text of the wikipedia articles related to the words on the board. She is considered the "Basic" Bot. Not a lot of fancy stuff going on with her but even though she may take her time on some turns, she will always perform well!

Ermis

Ermis tries to be a smarter and faster Bot. He only looks into the summaries of the wikipedia articles for his clues, supposedly that is where the most useful information lies. He also adapts to the number of words on the board in order to improve his performance but also maintain a consistent speed. He might be able to provide good clues pretty fast but sometimes he might prove to be a little too hasty!

Tantalus

Tantalus is the greediest of the Bots. He associates words in a similar way as Vasikia but chooses clues in a greedy manner. His main focus is to offer clues that are connected to as many words on the board as possible, in order to finish the game faster. this behaviour will pay off in a lot of the games but might prove risky at others!

Slow Vasikia

Slow Vasikia works exactly the same way as Vasikia. The only difference is that Slow Vasikia looks into more articles than Vasikia. That means she will take more time to find clues, but generally will provide better clues.
