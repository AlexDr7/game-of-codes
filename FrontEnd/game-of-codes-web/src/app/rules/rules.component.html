Rules

Overview

This game is played by two teams: Red and Blue.

The board consists of 25 words in a 5x5 grid.

Each team is consisted of one guide and players. During each turn the guide will provide clues to his team in order to help them guess which words on the board belong to their colour. The clues are given in the form of a single word accompanied with the number of words that it relates to.

After the clue is given the players of the team try to guess which words their guide is hinting at. They pick words one by one until they either choose a wrong word (a word not of their own colour) or until they guess the number of words specified by their guide.

Be careful though! If you guess the Purple word your team instantly loses!

Winning the game

To win the game your team must reveal all of the words of your own colour before all of the words of the opposing colour are revealed.

The Scoreboard on the top left shows the number of words left from each colour. Keep the number of words in mind while you are playing!

Giving Clues

As a guide your goal is to give a clue to your teammates with which they will be able to guess the larger number of words possible correctly. The clue must be a single word. When you have decided on a clue, you write the clue on the text-field, choose the number of words that it relates to and Press Give Clue to start the player's turn. The guides also have access to the Guide's View, which turns the words into their real colours.

Be careful! Only the two guides are allowed to look at the guides view! Players should not look at the screen when the Guide's View is active!

When giving clues keep in mind that your clue should under no circumstances be related to the Purple word, since your players might guess it and lose the game.

Guessing

As a player you try to relate the clue given from your guide to the words on the Board. When you want to guess a word, you simply click on it and the colour of the word is revealed. If the colour revealed was of your own team, you can keep guessing. Your turn ends when the word guessed is not your own, or when you have correctly guessed all the related words. If you choose a Purple word, you instantly lose the game. Grey words are neutral, if you pick them your turn is over, but no harm is done. Finally, if you choose a word of the opposing colour, you have just ended your turn and helped the enemy team.

Single Team Mode

Single Team Mode is designed for small groups of people. You play as the Blue team by default and each time your turn ends, a Red word is revealed. Your goal is to guess the Blue words in the fewest turns possible. If you succeed you get a score depending on how many you managed to get. This mode is perfect for single players combined with the use of an AI agent.

AI Agents

AI agents can be used in place of a team member. They can play as a Guide or a Player. If you want to play the game but there are not enough human players, you can use one or multiple AIs! Or you can add an AI agent on your team just for fun! The AI agents are thinking about the clues in real time as you do, so they will need some time to think! Read more about the agents on the About tab on the top right.
